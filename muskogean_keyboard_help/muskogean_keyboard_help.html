get those Muskogean orthography symbols you need...

If you're super smart and hip you probably already have a keyboard for whichever Muskogean language(s) you're interested in downloaded on your computer, but maybe you're like me and you've never gotten around to doing it.

Buttons to just copy straight to clipboard:

Type in this box to get a converted string of text

conversion key:

vv = ʋ (can do uppercase or lowercase)

r = ł (can do uppercase or lowercase)

a_ = a̱ (can add the accent to any letter)

a' = á (can add the accent to any letter)

a^ = â (can add the accent to any letter)
